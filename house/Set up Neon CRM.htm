Operation

The data only goes one way – from HHK to Neon CRM.  You can create new accounts, and update existing accounts.

Browse to the HHK Guest Tracking site and log in.

Under the “House” menu item along the top of the page, select “Guest Transfer”.

Once on the Guest Transfer page, you can search for names on either system:

·        Neon CRM Last Name Search:  Enter at least 3 letters of the last name.

·        Local (HHK) Name search:  Enter at least 3 letters of the first or last name.  When the name comes up, one of two buttons will appear depending upon if the Neon Id is set locally.

o   “Update Remote” – click to update Neon.

o   “Transfer to Remote” – click to send this new account to Neon.

Also, you can do bulk transfers of filtered HHK accounts to Neon CRM.

·        Set the Time Period to filter the HHK accounts.

·        Click “Get Records”

·        Click on the “Transfer” button when it appears

 

After clicking on “Transfer”, HHK will automatically transfer any new contacts to Neon CRM.  The Result column indicates what happened:

·        New Contact – record successfully transferred to Neon CRM

·        Previously Transferred – Neon CRM already has this contact.

·        Multiple Remote Matches – There are more than one match for the local contact.

·        Error message – Something is broken, record not transferred.
